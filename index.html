PERZona

PERZona is Lorenzo's solo project. All of his personal creative vision and artistry is embodied in this pseudonym of him. It is through this "double identity" where he is able to explore his musical vision at its full. Free from any rules or genre structure, PERZona is a means for Lorenzo's creative fullfilment. His music is a fusion of all his influences, mixing RnB, Neo Soul, Sound Design, Jazz and Indie. The vision behind this project is collaboration and community. Each song is a unique world where Lorenzo gets together with a different artist and begin to create something together. He has explored themes that revolve around self awareness, emotional introspetion, relationships, communication.
